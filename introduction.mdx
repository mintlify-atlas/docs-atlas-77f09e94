## What is Price Tracker Bot?

Price Tracker Bot is a Telegram bot that automatically monitors Amazon product prices and sends you instant notifications when prices drop. Never miss a deal again by tracking your favorite products and getting alerted the moment they go on sale.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add the bot to Telegram">
    Search for your deployed bot on Telegram and start a conversation with the `/start` command.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add products to track">
    Send the bot any Amazon product URL using the `/add` command, and it will immediately fetch the current price and details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive automatic alerts">
    The bot monitors your products every 2 hours. When a price drops, you'll get an instant notification with the savings amount and percentage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View analytics and manage">
    Use commands like `/list`, `/stats`, and `/chart` to view your tracked products, see statistics, and analyze price trends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Price Tracker Bot?

<Note>
Price Tracker Bot is designed to save you time and money by automating the tedious task of manually checking product prices. With smart notifications and detailed analytics, you'll always know the best time to buy.
</Note>

### Built with Modern Technology

The bot leverages cutting-edge technologies to provide reliable price tracking:

- **Node.js** - Fast and efficient runtime for real-time operations
- **Telegram Bot API** - Seamless integration with Telegram for instant notifications
- **Playwright** - Robust web scraping that works reliably with Amazon's dynamic pages
- **node-cron** - Scheduled tasks for automatic price monitoring
- **Persistent Storage** - Your tracked products are saved locally and never lost

<Tip>
The bot stores all price history data locally in `prices.json`, allowing you to maintain a complete record of price changes over time for better decision-making.
</Tip>
